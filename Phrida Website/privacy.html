Privacy Policy

Phrida does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users. The app operates entirely on your device and does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information.

What information do we collect?
None. Phrida does not collect or transmit any user data, analytics, or usage information. All your music and preferences remain private and local to your device.

Third-party services
Phrida does not use any third-party analytics or advertising SDKs that collect personal data. If you choose to remove ads via in-app purchase, this is handled securely by Apple and no purchase information is shared with us.

Contact
If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at app.phrida@icloud.com.
